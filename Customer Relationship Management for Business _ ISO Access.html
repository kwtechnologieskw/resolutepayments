ISO Access, designed specifically for merchant services and built for your business. Developed exclusively for the payment processing industry and increasing profitability and efficiency.

UNDERWRITING

Efficient underwriting tools to allow more applications to be processed and approved in a faster time-frame. Metrics reporting available for all steps in the procedure.

RESIDUAL CALCULATION

Import data from any processing platform, view, process and disburse residuals. Viewing rights control and sub-account setup hierarchy are powerful tools in larger companies.

TICKETING SYTEM

Advanced and multifaceted ticketing system to allow various support departments to assist sales reps and merchants with issues, updates or new business.

A fully functional and customized module to allow identification and tracking of inactive merchants and provide early warning to retain possible lost accounts.
