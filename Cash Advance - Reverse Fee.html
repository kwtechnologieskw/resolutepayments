Cash Advance

ReverseFee offers ReverseFee Cash Advance (RCA) solution Nationwide. Rather than a loan, the RCA is a cash advance based upon the credit card sales deposited in a business ReverseFee account. With an RCA, we advance you cash in exchange for a percentage of your daily credit card and debit card sales, plus a fee. An RCA is one of the faster financing options. This method of financing is one of the most innovative solutions in alternative business finance. In other words if your cash flow situation is positive, a ReverseMyFees Cash Advance can be a great solution to simple, secure and fast business finance to get your business on the right track.

Need Cash?

Our company offers a risk-free alternative method of ReverseFee funding.

How it Works:

ReverseFee offers a cash discount program that allows merchant to offer their customers an incentive to pay in cash or debit card purchases. If the card holder is not paying in cash, the discountwill not apply.

How to become eligible for merchant cash advance:

The ReverseFee funding process works through your credit-card terminal; therefore, in order to finalize the funding process, you will need ReverseFee to provide your credit card processing. The transition to ReverseFee will be seamless and will not stop your daily business in any way. The Company strongly believes that your business will grow. ReverseFee, considered to be one of the best among the cash advance companies across the US, offers this merchant cash advance solution to take business owners to the next level in their highly competitive markets.

Availability is based on the following criteria:

  • Your business is a restaurant, retail or service business.
  • Your business processes at least $4000/month in credit card sales.
  • Your company has been in business for more than one year, and you have at least one year left on your lease.
  • Your business does not have any open judgments or bankruptcies.
  • Some Internet business may qualify - (Please Inquire with our Funding Specialist)
